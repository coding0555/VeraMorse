Our Story

EcoShield was founded with a mission to make living spaces safe and mosquito-free. Our journey began when we realized that traditional repellents often lacked eco-friendliness and reliability.

We combined natural ingredients with modern technology to develop coils and electronic repellents that offer strong, long-lasting protection without harming the environment.

Today, EcoShield stands for trust, quality, and innovation in the world of mosquito defense. From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in household protection, we remain committed to our core values of safety, sustainability, and innovation.

Every product we create undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards for effectiveness and environmental responsibility. We're proud to protect millions of families worldwide from mosquito-borne diseases while minimizing our ecological footprint.
